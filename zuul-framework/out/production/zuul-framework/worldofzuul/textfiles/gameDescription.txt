Welcome to the game "Plastic Roads"
Your task is to collect as much plastic as possible and deliver it to the roadbuilder, 
and let the roadbuilder build road until you reach 100%. 
You can interract with people around the map and gain tips and knowlegde.
To get started you can use the commands: etc. 'go north/south/east/west', 'collect', 'give'
Type 'help' if you need help.
