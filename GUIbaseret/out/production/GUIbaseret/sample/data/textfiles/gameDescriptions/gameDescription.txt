Welcome to the game "Plastic Roads"
Your task is to collect as much plastic as possible and deliver it to the roadbuilder, 
and let the roadbuilder build road until you reach the left side of the room.
You can interact with people around the map and gain tips and knowledge.
To get started use the arrows to move around the screen, and space to interact with items/persons close to you.
Press the '?' button if you need help.
